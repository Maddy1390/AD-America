Step 1: Deploy to Netlify

To use Netlify Forms, you need to deploy your website to Netlify (it's free!):

  1. Go to netlify.com and sign up for free
  2. Connect your GitHub repository or drag & drop your website folder
  3. Deploy your site - Netlify will automatically detect your forms!

Step 2: Access Form Submissions

Once deployed, you can view form submissions in your Netlify dashboard:

  1. Go to your site dashboard on Netlify
  2. Click on "Forms" in the sidebar
  3. View all submissions, export to CSV, or set up email notifications

Step 3: Set Up Email Notifications (Optional)

Get notified via email when someone submits a form:

  1. In your Netlify dashboard, go to Forms ‚Üí Settings
  2. Add notification emails under "Form notifications"
  3. You can also set up Slack, webhook, or Zapier integrations

If you prefer to use GitHub Pages instead of Netlify:

  2. Sign up for Formspree and get your form endpoint
  3. Update the form action attributes to point to your Formspree URL
